# Elev8

## Vision

Elev8 is a premium multi-vendor lifestyle marketplace inspired by modern brands like Nike, Adidas, Puma, and One8.

The platform enables verified sellers to manage and sell premium products while providing customers with a modern shopping experience through secure payments, wishlists, offers, reviews, and personalized recommendations.

---
