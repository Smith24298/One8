## Important Realizations

Redis is not a database replacement.

Redis is a cache.

---

Order is historical.

Product is mutable.

---

Payment Service hides payment gateway implementation.

---

Never trust the client.

---

Microservices are business domains.

Not technical folders.

---

Every service owns its own data.

---

Business rules determine database design.

Not SQL.
